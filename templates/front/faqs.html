Frequently Asked Questions

Everyday, we receive a bunch of questions regarding our services via emails or chat forums. It's our pleasure to provide the best experience for our users and therefore, we have addressed most of the recurring issues here.

Feel free to contact us if you did not find your issue resolved.

What specifically does healthchecks do for me?

Healthchecks is a watchdog for your cron jobs. It's a web server that listens for pings from your cron jobs, plus a web interface. You may read our docs.

Is healthchecks free?

Healthchecks is open source, you can get the code on github. However, we won't mind if you bought us coffee, or subscribe to one of our paid plans to get amazing offers. You might as well like to go through our pricing table
